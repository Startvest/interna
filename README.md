## What is interna?

Interna is a solution designed to provide internship opportunities to every student, equip them with professional tools and provide a community of students who have undergone internships and students who are looking for internships. 

Professional tools we offer:
- Community of students and interns
- CV Generator
- Interview Prep
- Access to Remote Internships 
- Mentorship sessions
- P2P Messaging platform
- Special jobs from our partner firms

### Mission

We are bridging the gap between learning and the industry, by providing a community of interns sharing their experiences.

### Vision

Our grand vision is to make interna available to every student around the world, to ease the process of students getting an internship and to be the go-to platform for students looking to gain practical experiences in the industry as early as possible.
